Community Rules

Code of Conduct and Scope of it

This page defines the collaboration culture, values, and organizational principles that guide QB Networks, Masscollabs Services, and related communities.

Scope

Who this code applies to

This code of conduct file is licensed under the terms of GFDLv1.3 or any later.

This Code of Conduct applies to QB Networks, Masscollabs Services or in other words Masscollabs and Procyberian Systems Distribution in other words PSD organizations.

Principles

Organizational Values

Our New Organization Model

We changed our mindset and the organization model from BSD-style to GNU-style. Thus we are a better freedom respecting organization and more open, ready to collaborate with people all around the world.

Why this Code of Conduct matters

We are building a free, open, friendly, and collaborative community in other words a community for communities and a company for companies.

We need to express clearly what we are doing and why, so outsiders can understand us easily.

Core Values for Work Processes

Decentralized collaboration is key. Developers contribute independently from all around the world, while transparency and accessibility remain at the forefront.

Freedom and ethical values are indispensable principles, and community support through bug reports, documentation, and code contributions is essential.

Preferred Licenses

We prefer copyleft licenses. Mostly we prefer AGPLv3-or-later because we are focused on Internet technologies.

For other software GPLv3-or-later, for documentation GFDLv1.3-or-later, and for artworks CC BY-SA 4.0-or-later are what we prefer for our own works.

Core Values

Our Core Values

  • Freedom of speech
  • Openness
  • Collaborative working
  • Privacy of community members and customers
  • Four Freedoms written by Free Software Foundation

Happy Hacking

Culture and Public Good

Hacker culture is about exploring limits in a clever and useful way, much like jazz improvisation or martial arts culture.

  • Freedom is good
  • Openness is good
  • Collaboration is good
  • Privacy is good
  • We encourage good and useful things for the public.
